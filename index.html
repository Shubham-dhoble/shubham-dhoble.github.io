About the event

Lose yourself to the music and dance like no one’s watching. The Danse Studio: Time to Revolutionize You and MAXI - Marketing Association of XLRI present- Dance Mania, kids’ group dance competition.

Register your team as per the rules below and set the stage on fire with the oomph and pomp of your steps. We know that the steel city has many little masters!
